    ABOUT ME

    Born in Alberta, Canada and raised in South America and Texas has made me not only a real southern girl but also extremely cultured. My parents are from Hong Kong and I grew up speaking Cantonese. At a very young age I was exposed to English and French from Canada, Spanish and castillian Spanish from South America and Texas, and Cantonese and Mandarin Chinese from my family. In my 10 years as a public school teacher, I gathered essential skills in effective communication, motivation, project management, team building, multi-tasking, organization, and patience. Coming from the education world, I saw such a disparity between the technology that was available to the world and the technology that was being used in the classroom both as a teaching tool and implementation/organization tool. I was always interested in computer programming and I finally tool the jump in May of 2017 to the world of coding. I want to eventually merge my two passions to make a positive impact in the education world.
